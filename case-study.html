Case Study

Explore how Clem’s Nation brings ideas to life through creativity and precision. Each project tells a story of planning, design, and flawless execution — turning visions into unforgettable experiences.

Birthday Party at The Globe

When it’s a European Billionaire’s big birthday, there’s no expense spared! The event was attended by 150 guests and took place at The Underglobe, within the iconic Shakespeare’s Globe. Themed as a forested wild wonderland, we provided celebrity singers, outrageous performers, and flowers galore!

Brand Awareness Campaign

Can you create a unique and engaging brand experience through a digital marketing campaign? We arranged for eight of our client’s lucky customers to take a surprise trip to Belgium and learn Assisted Free Fall! The campaign resulted in thousands of new followers on the company’s Facebook page, and the real-life experience and social media exposure combined to create powerful brand awareness that resonated with their existing customers and beyond.

Captain Morgan Product Launch

A hugely successful launch party of our favourite tipple, Captain Morgan Spiced. G&D event planners created a tropical paradise in London to capture the brand’s (ahem) spirit. The immersive decor and attention to detail left an impactful impression on attendees. This resulted in a product launch that our clients and their very happy customers will never forget.

Company Milestone Event

We were overwhelmed to manage the party to celebrate Hello Magazine’s 1000th issue! The outrageously fun and festive party was attended by 400 guests including celebrities, editors, journalists, and loyal readers of the magazine. The unique and tailored event included an interactive wall projection and a viewing gallery showcasing past issues of the legendary and vital magazine.

Engagement House Party

A high-end engagement party for a special client who wanted to celebrate with close friends and family. G&D Party Planners transformed the client’s home into the ultimate party destination. It was an Ibiza-styled evening with nightclub lighting, bespoke cocktails, and lots of fabulous food. The party had the most flamboyant entertainers, including a fire dancer, a massage station, and a grand ice sculpture sushi bar.

Financial Services Awards Dinner

A glamorous awards dinner for a high-end Financial Services client at The Carlton Tower Jumeriah in Central London. From drinks reception to fine dining, entertainment, and awards ceremony. A very happy client, repeat business, and satisfied attendees

Global Beauty Brand 3-day Event

A globally-renowned beauty brand hosted a three-day ‘Tribe Event’ on the exclusive Osea Island, tucked away in the Blackwater Estuary of Essex, inviting over 400 stockists including therapists and salon owners. The goal? To provide an enriching platform for learning, development, and a bit of R&R.

High-End 21st Birthday Party

A high-end celebration to mark a 21st birthday, with the objective of finding a stunning venue, creating a fun-filled atmosphere, and securing an A-list artist to make it unforgettable. The client selected One Marylebone as the venue and transformed it into a tropical carnival. The night was a huge success, exceeding the client’s expectations. This celebration was brought to life by our creative birthday planners in London with every detail perfectly tailored.

Green Revolution Awards

A Green Revolution Gala dinner awards ceremony for 500 guests in Brighton. The event had a Berlin Wall-style set, extensive sound and lighting, and unique table centrepieces.

International Reunion UK Tour

A group of old classmates from Harvard Business School meet bi-annually to stay in touch and catch up on both business and personal life. Coming from all around the world, the location is chosen by a classmate who hosts the reunion in their home country. The host’s location for their 15th 7-day reunion is the United Kingdom and that’s where we come in.

Parties, weddings, concerts, or corporate gatherings, we create unforgettable moments filled with energy, style, and passion.
